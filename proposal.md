## Client Brief 

**Client:** Tamara Nascimento - Cuban-born, former nurse, devoted mother, and passionate home chef whose life journey spans resilience, war, migration, and the transformation of cooking into art and love. Through every chapter of her life, from Cuba to Angola to Brazil, the table has remained a place of expression, beauty, and connection.  

**Purpose:** To create a personal portfolio website that tells the story of Tamara's Journey: from her childhood in Cuba, to working as a nurse in Angola during the Civil War, and later rebuilding her life in Rio de Janeiro, where she chose to study gastronomy and later attend Le Cordon Bleu purely out of passion, because for her, cooking has always been an act of love.
